## Events

### Sansha Incursions (YC112)

In YC112, Sansha's Nation launched a sustained incursion cycle against the empires across high-sec, with market disruption recorded in Luminaire and other core systems. The show of force drew over 1,000 capsuleer pilots to Nation space in response. This was the first major sustained Sansha cycle of the modern era.

## Places

### Stain Region

Sansha's Nation's home region in southern null-sec. The industrial base of the True Power Project — captured slaves are processed into True Slaves here. Sansha's incursion campaigns reach outward into high-sec from this base of operations.

## Phenomena

### The Construct

A Jovian virtual reality and simulated environment that used time dilation to advance Jove civilization beyond physical-world limits. It was eventually rewired to use connected biological brains as a computational substrate.

**Cited in:** [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s)

### True Slaves

Sansha cybernetic forced-merger drones produced from captured slaves via the True Power Project. Sansha Kuvakei's signature technology and the primary product of the True Power Project.

### Vitoxin Slave-Control Chemistry

An Amarr slave-control compound that is fatally addictive — withdrawal kills. It maintains slave dependence on Amarr supply.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo) · [EVE: Templar One (Tony Gonzales)](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one)
