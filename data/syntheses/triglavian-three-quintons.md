## Events

### Hilen Tukoss Defection *(YC113)*

In YC113, Caldari scientist Hilen Tukoss defected, carrying Anoikis-research expertise out of Caldari corporate confinement. The defection is a pivotal moment in the Drifter / Sleeper / Construct research arc and prefigured the SoE / AIR Labs research consolidation that the Exordium charter eventually formalized. Tukoss was confirmed dead YC117.

### Triglavian Collective Revealed *(YC120)*

YC120 marked the first major public revelation of the Triglavian Collective as a coherent civilization rather than scattered abyssal anomalies, reported by the Scope. This came two years before the YC122 Triglavian Invasion that conquered Niarja and the 27 systems forming Pochven.

### Triglavian Invasion *(YC122)*

In YC122 the Triglavian Collective conquered 27 systems via the abyssal-filament invasion mechanic, forming Pochven, opening the Triglavian-Drifter conflict to capsuleer participation, and severing the Niarja high-sec corridor between Amarr and Caldari space. EDENCOM's formation as an inter-empire defensive coalition came directly from the Invasion response. Structurally the Invasion represented a third-power eruption between the four-empire system and Drifter forces.

### Fall of Niarja *(YC122)*

The YC122 Triglavian conquest of Niarja severed the high-sec trade corridor between Amarr and Caldari space, collapsing what was historically the most heavily-trafficked sub-cluster route. The Niarja severance is the structural cause of the Amarr-Caldari trade reorientation that Catiz I's territorial reorganization later codified.

### Drifter Crisis — Final Stages *(YC125)*

The YC125 events put five empire-and-collective occupiers in possession of Drifter pillar formations after Drifters were pushed out of wormhole space. Without this crisis there is no occupier list, no destructive-testing summit, and no Jita-gate. The access list it established underlies the Caldari accusation against the Gallente.

## Organizations

### AIR Laboratories

Ishukone-built research project. Major CONCORD funding shifted to AIR in what amounts to a Mountain/Ocean win against Forest. A former Sister of EVE sits on its leadership; ties to SoE wormhole research are structural, not coincidental.

---

### Society of Conscious Thought (SoCT)

Inter-empire research society that convenes capsuleer briefings on Drifter / Triglavian / Construct findings at Yulai. It functions as the structural bridge between the four empires on cosmic-scale research that no single empire wants to claim, and sits adjacent to AIR Labs and the Sisters of EVE Anoikis programs.

### Triglavian Collective

Abyssal humanoid civilization that emerged from Abyssal Deadspace via the Drifter Crisis upheavals. Conquered 27 systems through the Invasion arc to form Pochven and engaged in a multi-front conflict with the Drifters. Their proxis-class doctrine sits adjacent to Construct-derived technology without using it in the same way, positioning them as a third pole alongside Drifter and capsuleer civilizations.
