## Events

### Minmatar Rebellion (Great Rebellion)

The YC 80s slave revolt that broke Amarr rule over the Minmatar peoples and founded the modern Republic. The Ammatar Mandate's original split — Amarr-loyalist Nefantar versus Republic-loyalist Minmatar — dates to this cycle. Every Minmatar storyline traces back to the Rebellion.

### Bombardment of Starkmanir Prime

Idonis Ardishapur ordered sustained Tachyon siege-laser orbital bombardment of Starkmanir Prime in retaliation for the killing of Archon Ardishapur, leaving the tribe believed annihilated for centuries. The effective genocide lit a 250-year fuse to the Great Rebellion and stands as the founding grievance of the modern Republic. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo) · [Lost Stars](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/lost-stars)

### Sisters of EVE Reveal Starkmanir Survivors *(YC110)*

The Sisters of EVE publicly announced that a surviving Starkmanir population was alive on Hzeron in the Ammatar Mandate, forcing the hand of the Nefantar conspirators and triggering the Elder Fleet rescue operation. The reveal bridged the Old Wars to the Imperian Age and made the Ammatar-as-secret-protectors framing canonical. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Starkmanir Liberation (Imperian Age) *(YC110)*

The YC110 Elder Fleet operation liberated the Starkmanir from the Ammatar Mandate and restored them as a Minmatar tribe. It marks the previous occasion on which CONCORD's framework failed to constrain inter-empire violence over the Mandate. **Cited in:** [BREAKING NEWS! CALDARI INVADES THE SYNDICATE!!!! - EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) · [The Ammatars](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/the-ammatars)

### Republic Liberation Day *(annual)*

An annual Minmatar Republic holiday commemorating the original Rebellion. Schools, government, and Tribal Council operations all pause for the day. The Tribal Council uses Liberation Day for major proclamations. **Cited in:** [Republic stands still for Liberation Day (YC 119)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/republic-stands-still-as-trillions-prepare-for-liberation-day-celebrations/) · [Sanmatar Shakor address Liberation Day crowds (YC 120)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/sanmatar-maleatu-shakor-addresses-minmatar-liberation-day-crowds/) · [Galactic Hour News Roundup: Liberation Day (YC 122)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/galactic-hour-news-roundup-liberation-day-yc122-edition/)

## People

### Arzad Hamri

A liberal Amarr holder of Starkmanir who tolerated the tribe's heretical interpretation of the Amarr faith. Orthodox authorities executed him and replaced him with Archon Ardishapur — his removal is the proximate cause of the Starkmanir genocide. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Anaras Ult

Ammatar Consul-Governor at the time of the Elder Fleet attack and a Nefantar conspirator who actively enabled the mass Ammatar defection back to the Republic. He was the political face of the Nefantar long-game protection of the Starkmanir. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Jamal Sarum

Issued the post-Elder-Fleet proclamation welcoming the Starkmanir, Thukker, and Nefantar back into the Minmatar Republic — the legal-rhetorical act that made the tribal restoration canonical. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Karin Midular

Minmatar Sanmatar of the CONCORD-protest era. She pursued peace and appeasement with the Amarr — a track controversial within the Republic because it sidelined the still-enslaved Minmatar population. ## Organizations

### Tribal Council

The Minmatar Republic's governing body, comprising the tribal chiefs of all seven tribes — Brutor, Sebiestor, Krusual, Vherokior, Nefantar, Starkmanir, and Thukker — under the Sanmatar's chairmanship. It approved Upwell workforce transfers post-conquest and is the operational layer that gives the Republic its coalition pluralism. **Cited in:** [Tribal Council calls for candidates to apply to Republic Fleet capsuleer program (YC 118)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/minmatar-tribal-council-calls-for-candidates-to-apply-to-republic-fleet-capsuleer-recruitment-program/) · [Minmatar Tribal Council Calls for Candidates to Apply to Republic Fleet Capsuleer Recruitment Program](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/minmatar-tribal-council-calls-for-candidates-to-apply-to-republic-fleet-capsuleer-recruitment-program) · [Minmatar Tribal Council to begin February 14th, Tribal reps to make historic convoy to Matar on 13th](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/minmatar-tribal-council-to-begin-february-14th-tribal-reps-to-make-historic-convoy-to-matar-on-13th) · [Tribal Council Approves Upwell Workforce Transfers](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/tribal-council-approves-upwell-workforce-transfers) · [Tribal Council delegates arrive in Pator amidst fanfare, fireworks, and hostilities](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/tribal-council-delegates-arrive-in-pator-amidst-fanfare-fireworks-and-hostilities)

### Vherokior Tribe

One of the seven Minmatar tribes restored by the Elder War cycle, defined by a spiritual and mystic cultural identity within the Republic's tribal pluralism.
