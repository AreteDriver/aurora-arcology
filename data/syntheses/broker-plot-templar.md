## Events

### Malkalen Disaster *(YC110)*

At the YC110 Caldari–Federation summit aboard Ishukone Headquarters station at Malkalen, Alexander Noir's Nyx struck the station with safety protocols disabled, killing Otro Gariushi and tens of thousands. The broker plot targeted Otro specifically to stop Ishukone's Insorum development. The disaster became Tibus Heth's pretext to militarize the State and triggered the YC112 Caldari invasion of Caldari Prime — the largest interstate war of the modern era.

### Aidonis Award to Alexander Noir *(YC110)*

Alexander Noir received the Aidonis peace-prize award in YC110 — the same year his Nyx mothership struck Ishukone HQ at Malkalen and killed Otro Gariushi. Noir was being publicly honored as the Federation's peace champion at the moment the broker plot used him for the assassination. Whether Noir was set up or willingly participated is canonically unresolved.

**Cited in:** [Admiral Alexander Noir receives the Aidonis (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/admiral-alexander-noir-receives-the-aidonis/)

### Death of Otro Gariushi *(YC110)*

At the YC110 Caldari–Federation summit, Alexander Noir's Nyx hit the station with safety protocols disabled — not the act of a normal pilot. The broker plot targeted Otro because Ishukone was developing Insorum, the Vitoxin antidote that threatened the structural basis of Amarr slavery. Mens Reppola inherited the CEO seat and the Insorum mandate.

**Cited in:** [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (recorded YC126.04.04)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [EVE: The Empyrean Age (Tony Gonzales)](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/the-empyrean-age)

### Caldari Invasion of Caldari Prime *(YC112)*

The YC112 Caldari State invasion retook Caldari Prime from Federation control. Tibus Heth justified the operation as restitution for the Malkalen Disaster. Mordu's Legion participated under contract, then later announced departure. The invasion locked in Heth's Provist regime and established the modern Caldari claim on Caldari Prime.

## Persons

### Alexander Noir *(YC110)*

Capsuleer pilot of the Nyx mothership that struck Ishukone Headquarters at Malkalen, killing Otro Gariushi and tens of thousands. Officially named the perpetrator of the Malkalen Disaster, but the broker plot evidence — station and ship safety protocols disabled simultaneously — suggests he was set up to take the blame for the Insorum-targeted assassination. The structural ambiguity around Noir is what makes Malkalen canonically a 'Disaster' rather than an 'Attack.' Noir was receiving the Aidonis peace-prize in YC110, the same year as the strike; whether he was framed or complicit remains unresolved.

### Tibus Heth *(YC108–YC115)*

The most disruptive Caldari political figure of the modern era. Heth's reclamation of Caldari Prime turned populist anger into a cross-corp alliance — the Provists — that swept aside the post-rebellion mega-corp consensus. Under Heth, Ytiri was absorbed into the alliance, Sukuuvestaa pushed back, and the State briefly operated as a single political bloc. His fall around YC115 left a power vacuum that the Mountain and Ocean factions filled, completing the Lai Dai-vs-Ishukone realignment.
