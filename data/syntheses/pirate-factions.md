## Events

### Skarkon Angel Cartel Takeover (~YC110)

Around YC110, the Angel Cartel took control of the Minmatar system of Skarkon. Locals voted to join the Cartel and shut their stargates against the Republic — a direct symptom of Republic governance weakness during the Midular era. [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Archangels Annex and Withdraw from Skarkon (YC110)

In YC110, the Angel Cartel sub-faction Archangels formally declared annexation of Skarkon, then withdrew the claim under Republic counter-pressure. ## Organizations

### Angel Cartel

Southern New Eden's enduring pirate organization, operating from Curse, Great Wildlands, and Stain-adjacent space. Angel Cartel financing has been documented in multiple Caldari–Gallente political destabilization events; the four-empire posture toward them is performatively hostile but structurally tolerant. Mordu's Legion's founding membership came from Angel Cartel deserters, making the relationship between the two more genealogical than antagonistic. Skarkon's defection to Cartel administration around YC110 is the canonical demonstration of how an empire-region falls when the Republic stops servicing it.
