## Events

### Hilen Tukoss Defection *(YC113)*

In YC113, Caldari scientist Hilen Tukoss defected, carrying Anoikis-research expertise out of Caldari corporate confinement. The defection was a pivotal moment in the Drifter / Sleeper / Construct research arc, and it prefigured the SoE / AIR Labs research consolidation that the Exordium charter eventually formalized. His death was confirmed by DED in YC117.

### Capsuleer Day *(established YC117)*

An annual capsuleer-recognition holiday established in YC117 by the Upwell Consortium. Capsuleer Day XXIII falls in YC128 — the first such observance during a CONCORD-system breach since the YC110 Yulai gate event.

### Capsuleer Training Relocation to Exordium *(YC128)*

AIR-managed training programs — which AIR took over in YC123 — relocated to Exordium. New capsuleers now begin their careers in a no-violence, CONCORD-protected region rather than in the Caldari corporate-state milieu, structurally weakening the Caldari training-as-recruitment lever the State has historically exploited.

**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Exordium Charter / Yulai Accord Amendment *(YC128-04-13)*

On YC128-04-13, the CONCORD Inner Circle and AIR Laboratories co-signed an amendment to the Yulai Accords establishing the new region of Exordium with no-violence security protocols. The public ceremony was held in orbit around Yulai 8. The charter connects directly to AIR Labs' rising institutional profile and to the CONCORD-funded research thread that had previously flowed toward EDENCOM.

**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(YC128-04-30)*

On YC128-04-30, CONCORD's war-response system was disabled by a cyberattack of unprecedented complexity. Within minutes, all four empires had launched cross-incursions into each other's high-security space — the first such breach since the Yulai gate event of YC110. A single message accompanied the attack: *"Warpath, a gift dear empires. After all, toys are meant to be played with."* The origin of the attack remains unattributed.

## Organizations

### AIR Laboratories

An Ishukone-built research project into which major CONCORD funding shifted — a reallocation that came at the expense of EDENCOM. A former Sister of EVE sits on its leadership, making ties to SoE wormhole research structural rather than incidental. AIR took over capsuleer training in YC123 and subsequently relocated those programs to Exordium.

---

### CONCORD Inner Circle

The inter-empire diplomatic body headed by Aras Angarae. It co-signed the Exordium Charter with AIR Laboratories and subsequently convened an emergency session following the Jita-gate incident, which ended early when all parties walked out.
