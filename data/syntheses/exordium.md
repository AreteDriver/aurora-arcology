# Exordium / AIR / Capsuleer Future

> AIR Laboratories, Yulai Accord amendment, the new Exordium region, capsuleer training relocation, Catiz's role.

*Source-grounded synthesis. Curator-authored claim text, deduplicated. Every citation traces to a primary source.*

## Events

### Capsuleer Training Relocation to Exordium *(2026-04)*

Structural shift in the new-pilot pipeline. From day one of their career, capsuleers will be physically in AIR-managed CONCORD-protected space — not the Caldari corp-state milieu. Long-tail consequences: changes the cultural baseline of new pilots, weakens the Caldari training-as-recruitment lever the State has historically exploited.
**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Exordium Charter (Yulai Accord Amendment) *(2026-04-13)*

The constitutional event for the Exordium region. CCP's first canonical move toward a deconfliction zone — CONCORD and AIR jointly chartered a 50-system region with capsuleer-side safety enforced by Yulai Accord amendment. Connects directly to AIR Labs' rising profile in the Mountain/Ocean realignment, and to the Drifter-experimentation policy thread (the CONCORD-funded research that would otherwise have flowed to EDENCOM).
**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(2026-04-30)*

The structural fracture of the YC128 cycle. CONCORD's war-response automation collapsed under a precise cyberattack, allowing all four empires to invade each other's high-sec for the first time since the Yulai event of YC110. Marks the moment the high-sec social contract stopped being a structural guarantee and became a narrative one. Origin remains unattributed; the message text reads as Guristas-flavored.

## Persons

### Aldeya Balasharian

AIR's leadership face during the Exordium era. Announced AIR's super-stable warp drive — the technical primitive that made building a new region in years rather than decades possible. Ties AIR Labs to a named principal that connects upward to the Mountain/Ocean realignment Ash mapped on his whiteboard.
**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Aras Angarae

The diplomatic face of CONCORD across the YC128 cycle. Signed the Exordium amendment with Balasharian, then chaired the post-Jita-gate session that ended with all parties walking out. Two consecutive multi-empire signatures bracketing the Warpath Protocol Failure.
**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

## Organizations

### AIR Laboratories

Ishukone-built research project. Major CONCORD funding shifted to AIR around the same time — a Mountain/Ocean win against Forest. A former Sister of Eve sits on its leadership; ties to SoE wormhole research are structural, not coincidental.

### Amarr Imperial Navy

Amarr Empire military fleet. Operates across all five Royal Houses' territories under the Empress's direct authority. Currently engaged in operations against Drifters in Khanid space and against capsuleer disruption of slave-shipment lanes — and Catiz I's territorial reorganization restructured their command chain.

### Exordium

Aurora's first canonical neutral space. Strategic significance: the only region in New Eden with capsuleer-violence protocols structurally enforced. The technical primitive that made Exordium feasible in human-scale time. ## Concepts

### Emergency Militia War Powers Act

Emergency Militia War Powers Act. CONCORD act that established Faction Warfare. Cited by Kantar Yun in the YC120 Kahah crisis as having been violated repeatedly in highsec without consequence — the precedent CONCORD's Warpath failure now repeats at scale.

### Yulai Accords

The constitutional document of CONCORD-era New Eden. Every multi-empire structural framework — EMWPA, Exordium charter, Warpath Protocol — extends or amends the Yulai Accords. Warpath Protocol Failure is, in this reading, a breach of the same accords being amended to create Exordium two weeks earlier.
