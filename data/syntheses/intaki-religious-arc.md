## Events

### Amarr Reclaiming

The religious-imperial expansion that built the Amarr Empire through the subjugation of neighboring peoples. The Minmatar conquest is its climactic chapter; the Khanid secession its first major failure; the Battle of Vak'Atioth — when the Jove repulsed Amarr expansion — its eventual ceiling. Every Amarr-driven storyline traces back to Reclaiming theology.

### Salvation Crusade *(YC111)*

Minmatar preacher Jarek launched a religious mobilization that crossed from Amarr space into Republic territory. Crusaders established camps in Pator, encountered hostile ships in Heimatar with firefight averted by mercenary intervention, and drew Gallente medical charities into the controversy. Rioters eventually stormed a Crusade camp on Matar and burned its chapel to the ground. Jarek himself died in an attack on a Crusade township.

### Lost Adama Discovery *(YC120)*

Ida monk Negui Erlon claimed that an Intaki slave named Eloa Enaro, held aboard the Khanid cruiser *Pure Faith Harvest*, was a reincarnated Adama who had vanished in YC99. Khanid Sebiros Atsa Kufair refused to sell or surrender her.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Kahah Chemical Attacks *(YC120)*

Coordinated weaponized Death Glow and Insorum strikes across the Kahah system triggered slave revolts. The Khanid response, directed by Sebiros Allar Chakaide, killed somewhere between 300,000 and 9 million people depending on the estimate. Chakaide openly accused the Sisters of Eve and the Mimitar of orchestrating the attacks; the Mimitar countered that Chakaide himself had Blood Raider ties — neither claim has been resolved canonically.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Pure Faith Harvest Raid *(YC120)*

Intaki extremists assaulted the Khanid cruiser holding Eloa Enaro near a stargate at Vasala. The Royal Khanid Navy repulsed the raid. Federation and Republic sanctions against the Amarr Empire followed.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Federation Seizure of Intaki *(YC124)*

The Federation aggressively removed Intaki and two adjacent constellations from the Faction Warfare war zone and made them full Federation property. This triggered the Uprising arc and the original Caldari–Intaki Syndicate constellation deal.

**Cited in:** [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (recorded YC126.04.04)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI)

## People

### Negui Erlon

Ida monk whose claim that Eloa Enaro was a reincarnated lost Adama turned a slave-purchase dispute into a religious-extremist flashpoint.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Eloa Enaro

Intaki slave aboard the *Pure Faith Harvest*, claimed by the Ida faith to be a reincarnated Adama who had vanished in YC99.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Atsa Kufair

Khanid Sebiros and owner of the *Pure Faith Harvest*. Refused to sell or surrender Eloa Enaro despite the Ida monk's claims, making him the proximate cause of the Pure Faith Harvest raid.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Allar Chakaide

Khanid Sebiros, Plenipotentiary, and commander of the 19th Royal Uolans. Led the brutal YC120 Kahah pacification. Openly hostile to the Ammatar; accused the Sisters of Eve and the Mimitar of orchestrating the chemical attacks.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Jarek *(YC111)*

Minmatar preacher who led the YC111 Salvation Crusade across the Amarr–Republic border, sustaining the movement through Heimatar firefights until his death in an attack on a Crusade township.

## Organizations

### Ida Faith / Ida Monastery

The Intaki religion. Believes in reincarnation; Adamas reincarnate without technology. The structural source of Intaki political-cultural distinctiveness inside the Federation — and the belief that radicalized Intaki extremists in the YC120 Lost Adama cycle.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Intaki Syndicate

Loose autonomous federation in the Syndicate region, founded by Caldari-sympathetic Intaki exiles after the Caldari Rebellion. Each station is formally autonomous; the Syndicate is officially non-political. The YC128.05.05 Caldari conquest ended its sovereignty, though its membership network continued operating — with capital ships escaping into highsec — as a fugitive financial and smuggling structure.

**Cited in:** [Silphy en Diabel Arrested in Relation to Jita-gate Incident — New Eden News](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLp1gEBqEw) · [The Scope — Intaki Syndicate Fleet Attempts Breakout](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfqboHJUNpw)

### Royal Khanid Navy

The Khanid Kingdom's autonomous military fleet, separate from the Imperial Navy. Repulsed the Pure Faith Harvest raid in YC120 and conducted the Kahah pacification under Allar Chakaide. The 19th Royal Uolans is one of its elite units.

**Cited in:** [Royal Khanid Navy releases statement on Blood Raider attacks](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/royal-khanid-navy-releases-statement-on-blood-raider-attacks)

### Federal Defense Union

Gallente Federation Faction Warfare militia — the Federation's volunteer and capsuleer-pilot frontline against the State in the FW war zone. Operates under Federation Navy authority.

**Cited in:** [First Federal Defense Union carrier killed (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/first-federal-defense-union-carrier-killed/)

### Federation Senate

Federation governance body. A hawkish versus Autonomist factional split surfaces in the Moreau era, with hawks concerned the Autonomist president cannot direct the Federation Navy firmly.
