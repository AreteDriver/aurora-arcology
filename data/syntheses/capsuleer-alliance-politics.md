## Events

### Capsuleer Day (annual) *(YC117+)*

Annual capsuleer-recognition holiday established YC117 by Upwell Consortium. Capsuleer Day XXIII (YC128) coincides with the Warpath Protocol failure cycle — the first Capsuleer Day during a CONCORD-system breach since the YC110 Yulai gate event.

**Cited in:** [Upwell Consortium announces capsuleer day (YC 118)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/upwell-consortium-announces-capsuleer-day/) · [Galactic Hour News Roundup: Capsuleer Day (YC 121)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/galactic-hour-news-roundup-capsuleer-day-yc121-edition/)

### Capsuleer Training Relocation to Exordium *(YC128-04)*

AIR-managed training programs — which AIR took over in YC123 — relocated to Exordium. From the first day of their career, new capsuleers will be physically situated in AIR-managed, CONCORD-protected space rather than the Caldari corp-state milieu. Long-tail consequences include changes to the cultural baseline of new pilots and a weakening of the Caldari training-as-recruitment lever the State has historically exploited.

**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Capsuleer Day XXIII — Warpath *(YC128-05)*

The framing event that bundles the four-empire incursion arc, the Syndicate conquest, and the capital breakout into a single live-narrative payload. Encompasses Warpath cross-empire incursions, Syndicate conquest, capital breakout, and capital-doomsday site rollout. Run-up to Fanfest YC128, where the Military Campaigns expansion will use this arc as its launch pad.

### Soraya Roden

Election runner-up. Roden Shipyards heir; Quafe-sponsored; hawkish military posture. Would have been the predictable response to Caldari escalation — Moreau's election denied Lai Dai and Forest the Federation hawk they likely backed.

### Caroline Grace *(YC116)*

Capsuleer who detected the YC116 stellar anomaly that became known as Caroline's Star — the visible signature of the Drifter network reaching New Eden's normal space. Her detection is the moment the Drifter conflict moved from Anoikis backstory to public phenomenon.

### Federal Defense Union

Gallente Federation Faction Warfare militia — the Federation's volunteer and capsuleer-pilot frontline against the Caldari State in the FW war zone. Operates under Federation Navy authority.

### Mercenary Coalition (capsuleer alliance)

Major capsuleer mercenary alliance. Lost a Ragnarok-class Titan in YC109 capsuleer-versus-capsuleer combat — the precedent event that established Titan-class ships as feasible objectives for coordinated capsuleer-alliance operations. Continues to operate as a hireable mercenary-alliance faction.

### Northern Coalition (capsuleer alliance)

Major capsuleer-alliance bloc. A northern null-sec power organized after the YC106 capsuleer-alliance recognition, operating across Pure Blind, Cloud Ring, Tribute, and Vale of the Silent. One of the structural counterweights to Goonswarm in the modern null-sec balance.

### Paxton Federation (capsuleer alliance)

Amarr-loyalist capsuleer alliance providing imperial-aligned operational support — one of the capsuleer alliances that furnishes political validation for the Empire's claim to capsuleer-faction allegiance.
