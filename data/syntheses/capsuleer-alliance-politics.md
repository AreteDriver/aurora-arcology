# Capsuleer Alliance Politics

> The null-sec power-bloc layer: YC106 capsuleer-alliance recognition, Goonswarm Erebus destruction, Mercenary Coalition Ragnarok loss, Ushra'Khan's Imperial Armageddon capture, the Northern Coalition / Paxton blocs, and capsuleer political influence on empire elections.

*Source-grounded synthesis. Curator-authored claim text, deduplicated. Every citation traces to a primary source.*

## Events

### Capsuleer Day (annual) *(YC117+)*

Annual capsuleer-recognition holiday established YC117 by Upwell Consortium. Capsuleer Day XXIII (YC128) coincides with the Warpath Protocol failure cycle — the first Capsuleer Day during a CONCORD-system breach since the YC110 Yulai gate event. The recognition cycle for capsuleer political importance; structural ancestor of the YC128 Warpath capsuleer mobilization.
**Cited in:** [Upwell Consortium announces capsuleer day (YC 118)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/upwell-consortium-announces-capsuleer-day/) · [Galactic Hour News Roundup: Capsuleer Day (YC 121)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/galactic-hour-news-roundup-capsuleer-day-yc121-edition/)

### Capsuleer Training Relocation to Exordium *(2026-04)*

Structural shift in the new-pilot pipeline. From day one of their career, capsuleers will be physically in AIR-managed CONCORD-protected space — not the Caldari corp-state milieu. Long-tail consequences: changes the cultural baseline of new pilots, weakens the Caldari training-as-recruitment lever the State has historically exploited.
**Cited in:** [CONCORD and AIR Announce New Space Region - New Eden News - YC128-04-13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhTUqEmgX5k)

### Capsuleer Day XXIII — Warpath *(2026-05)*

The framing event that bundles the four-empire incursion arc, the Syndicate conquest, and the capital breakout into a single live-narrative payload. Run-up to Fanfest YC128, where the Military Campaigns expansion will use this arc as its launch pad.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21 - YC128-04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [BREAKING NEWS! ## Persons

### Soraya Roden

Election runner-up. Roden Shipyards heir; Quafe-sponsored; hawkish military posture. Would have been the predictable response to Caldari escalation — Moreau's election denied Lai Dai/Forest the Federation hawk they likely backed.
**Cited in:** [BREAKING NEWS! ### Caroline Grace *(YC116)*

Capsuleer who detected the YC116 stellar anomaly that became known as Caroline's Star — the visible signature of the Drifter network reaching New Eden's normal space. Her detection is the moment the Drifter conflict moved from Anoikis backstory to public phenomenon.
