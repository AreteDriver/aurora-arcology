## Events

### Crielere Project (collapse) *(YC105)*

The [Crielere Project](https://www.eveonline.com/article/the-state-and-federation-open-new-research-center-1) was announced as a milestone in Caldari–Federation cooperation. [Caldari police forces came under investigation](https://www.eveonline.com/article/caldari-police-forces-in-crielere-are-currently-under-investigation-after-serious-accusations.) after sabotage and vandalism accusations, and the project collapsed amid intra-State factional conflict. [Dignitaries attending the Crielere conference](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/caldari-and-gallente-dignitaries-attend-conference-at-crielere-foiritan-causes-stir/) witnessed Foiritan cause a diplomatic stir; [Ishukone CEO Gariushi unveiled Crielere discoveries](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/ishukone-ceo-gariushi-unveils-crielere-discoveries/) shortly before [valuable blueprints went missing from the facility](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/valuable-blueprints-missing-from-the-crielere-research-facility/). The project's collapse foreshadowed the Mountain/Ocean axis split: pro-cooperation factions (later Ishukone-aligned) against anti-Federation factions (later Provist/Heth-aligned).

### Innovaatio Varustukset Scandal *(YC109)*

A YC109 espionage scandal at the Caldari military-development corp Innovaatio Varustukset ended with [sale and ruin](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/espionage-scandal-causes-innva-ruin/). The [laboratory was robbed](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-varustukset-laboratory-robbed/) and [scientists were kidnapped before being rescued](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-varustukset-scientists-kidnapped-rescued/). A [multi-corp bidding war involving Kaalakiota, Wiyrkomi, and Sukuuvestaa](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-sells-to-ishukone-for-surprising-sum/) exposed the Heth-era Provist consolidation pattern of state-favored megacorp absorption.

### Caldari Constructions Workers Revolt *(YC110)*

Tibus Heth, a foreman-turned-revolutionary, led a [labor-organizing coup at Caldari Constructions](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/breaking-news-worker-revolt-underway-at-caldari-constructions-facilities-1/). The [Mercantile Club hosted discussion on the takeover](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/mercantile-club-hosts-discussion-on-caldari-constructions-takeover/), and the [Federation cast a wary eye at the Heth situation](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/federation-casts-a-wary-eye-at-the-heth-situation/). This is the structural origin of Heth's rise — pre-Malkalen, pre-Empyrean Age. [Caldari Constructions share prices later recovered](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/caldari-constructions-share-prices-rise-on-reports-of-economic-recovery/) after Heth's fall, when the company moved to [nullify his shares](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/caldari-constructions-to-nullify-heths-shares/).

### Death of Otro Gariushi (Quantum Rise summit) *(YC110)*

At the YC110 Caldari–Federation summit, [a Federation Nyx rammed the Ishukone headquarters station](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/update-federation-nyx-rams-ishukone-headquarters-station), killing Otro Gariushi. Safety protocols had been disabled — not the work of a normal pilot. The broker plot targeted Otro specifically because Ishukone was developing Insorum, the Vitoxin antidote that threatened the structural basis of Amarr slavery. [Ishukone confirmed Gariushi's death](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/ishukone-statement-otro-gariushi-confirmed-dead); Mens Reppola inherited the CEO seat and the Insorum mandate. ([EVE: The Empyrean Age](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/the-empyrean-age) · [Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI))

### Ishukone Wins Intaki Development Rights *(YC111)*

Following the Caldari system auction, [Ishukone secured development rights to the Intaki system](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-ishukone-wins-development-rights-to-intaki-system), anchoring the Ishukone–Intaki structural relationship that Silphy later inherited and exploited via the Antagonists chronicle. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI))

### Zephyr Ship Distribution *(YC111)*

The Intaki Syndicate mass-distributed the Zephyr exploration ship to all registered capsuleers. Sleeper-ignored, the vessel allowed capsuleers to map wormhole space — and quietly funneled telemetry data back through the Syndicate to Ishukone. The Zephyr is the original instance of Syndicate-as-data-conduit, structurally identical to the data flows that later sit inside Upwell. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Ante](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante))

## People

### Gable (codename: Lifegiver)

Caldari-born scientist converted to Amarrian faith. She treats the Templar prototype — the only patient with full hippocampus and thalamus cybernetic replacement — making her a structural bridge between Ishukone clone-tech and Amarr/Khanid theological politics. Identified as the 'Lifegiver' figure surfacing in YC128 events. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [EVE: Templar One](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one))

### Marcus Jror

Templar protagonist of Tony Gonzales's novels. Caldari capsuleer-clone-soldier hybrid and the clone-soldier program's first widely documented operative. Operates within the Mens Reppola / Muryia Mordu / Insorum nexus, protecting Insorum production at Mens's mandate. The canonical narrative instance of clone-soldier tech. ([EVE: Templar One](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one))

### Mens Reppola

Ishukone CEO since Otro Gariushi's death; the structural anchor of the Ishukone axis. Working with Mordu's Legion and Muryia Mordu on clone-soldier development, and operating under heavy drone protection — in semi-hiding by the end of *Templar One*. Every major Ishukone-axis claim in the YC128 cycle routes through him. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Mens Reppola Made Ishukone CEO, Charts Future Course](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/mens-reppola-made-ishukone-ceo-charts-future-course) · [Mens Reppola Announces 5% Stake In Ishukone](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/mens-reppola-announces-5-stake-in-ishukone) · [Federation Policymakers Exhibit Muted Reactions to Mens Reppola Appointment](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/federation-policymakers-exhibit-muted-reactions-to-mens-reppola-appointment) · [EVE: Templar One](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one) · [Ante](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante))

### Miller Reppola

Mens's sister and Ishukone CFO. Helped Otro engineer Akelan's removal via blackmail of Riley Rashemo, and operates as Mens's right hand during the post-summit reconstruction. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [EVE: Templar One](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one))

### Mr. Lacante

Likely Thukker (facial tattoos). In Silphy's Antagonists chronicle he coordinates the Zephyr telemetry-data trade with Ishukone — described as the kind of token Ishukone won't be able to resist. The mechanism by which Syndicate intelligence gets monetized via the Mountain/Ocean axis. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Ante](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante))

### Muryia Mordu

Founder of [Mordu's Legion](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/mordus-legion). Independent mercenary commander operating outside the four-empire structure. In the Antagonists chronicle, the 'mercenaries' Silphy negotiates around when offering Zephyr-telemetry data to Ishukone implicitly point to Mordu's people — Mens won't take the deal directly while they're in the way. Mordu's Legion's separate relationship to Ishukone via the Templar One clone-soldier program ties him structurally to the Mens Reppola axis. ([Muryia Mordu issues Operation Highlander Decoration](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/muryia-mordu-issues-operation-highlander-decoration) · [Muryia Mordu arrives safely at his headquarters; region in uproar](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/muryia-mordu-arrives-safely-at-his-headquarters-region-in-uproar) · [EVE: Templar One](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one) · [Ante](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante))

### Otro Gariushi

Former Ishukone CEO with Guristas-aligned pilot history. Rose to power by exposing Akelan's corruption via the Raven project, with Miller Reppola and Riley Rashemo helping engineer the takeover. Killed at the YC110 Caldari–Federation summit when Alexander Noir's Nyx struck the station — the broker wanted him dead specifically because Ishukone was developing Insorum. ([Silphy — The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Otro Gariushi defends Dark Amarr deal — claims chips improve slaves' lives](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/otro-gariushi-defends-dark-amarr-deal-claims-chips-improve-slaves-lives) · [ISHUKONE STATEMENT: OTRO GARIUSHI CONFIRMED DEAD](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/ishukone-statement-otro-gariushi-confirmed-dead) · [EVE: The Empyrean Age](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/the-empyrean-age))

### Raleigh

Ishukone's top engineer. Built the android Vilamo and is developing cybernetic implant tech — hippocampus and thalamus replacement — for Templar prototypes. The technical engine behind Ishukone's clone-soldier program.
