# Drifter / Hyperspace / Catalyst Arc

> Drifter Crisis aftermath, occupier-faction experimentation, AMP warning, Jita-gate weaponization, prismatic sites, hollow-clone disaster.

*Source-grounded synthesis. Curator-authored claim text, deduplicated. Every citation traces to a primary source.*

## Events

### Dread Attack at Tanu II

One of the YC123 dread assaults. Illegal high-sec cyno beacon enabled a dread fleet to land at the Ammatar Mandate's capital planet. The same tech precedent now powers the YC128 Syndicate breakout — the Tanu attack is the structural ancestor of the Catalyst-era doomsday rollout.
**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### YC123 Dread Assaults (Tanu / Junfaa) *(2021)*

Mercenary fleets used illegal high-sec-authorized cyno beacons to land dread assaults at Tanu and Junfaa. Established canonical existence of empire-circumventing cyno tech. Tanu II is the capital of the Ammatar Mandate — the same site Ash predicts as Mil-Campaign #2 zone.
**Cited in:** [BREAKING NEWS! CALDARI INVADES THE SYNDICATE!!!! - EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0)

### Drifter Crisis (final stages) *(2024)*

The YC125 events that put the five empire-and-collective occupiers in possession of Drifter pillar formations. Without this crisis there is no occupier list, no destructive-testing summit, and no Jita-gate. Establishes the access list that lawyers the Caldari accusation against Gallente.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI)

### Drifter Wormhole Summit *(2025)*

The YC126 summit at Jita IV-4 where the five wormhole occupiers (Amarr, Caldari, EDENCOM, Minmatar, Triglavian) jointly committed to destructive testing of the Drifter hyperspace network. Direct ancestor of the Jita-gate weaponization — they collectively published the cookbook.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI)

### AMP Raises Drifter Experiment Warnings *(2025-10-30)*

Rio Talifo and the Anoyus Mapping Project went public with concrete telemetry showing Drifter wormhole instability spilling into known space. Saraki dismissed it as Federation propaganda. Vindicated by Jita-gate; vindicated again by the Tanu II / Catalyst-era prismatic sites.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI) · [Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/warnings-raised-over-drifter-wormhole-experiments)

### Jita Gate Incident *(2026-04-21)*

The triggering catastrophe. Hyperspace fractures around all seven gates killed thousands and turned Jita into a politically untenable hub for the Caldari State. Crucially, the mechanism — manipulation of Drifter Hyperspace Network data layers — is only accessible to the five wormhole occupiers. That technical fact contradicts the Caldari accusation against the Gallente and forces the conspiracy outward toward Amarr / EDENCOM / Triglavian co-conspirators.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21 - YC128-04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [BREAKING NEWS! CALDARI INVADES THE SYNDICATE!!!! - EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) · [Gates into Jita Damaged As Tensions Between Empires Rise](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/gates-into-jita-damaged-as-tensions-between-empires-rise)

### Eve Gate Collapse *(861-AD)*

The break point between proto-history and modern New Eden. Every modern faction descends from a colony cut off by this event. Sisters of Eve research is fundamentally an attempt to understand and possibly reopen the gate. The Drifters' hyperspace network — the same one weaponized for Jita-gate — is structurally the heir to whatever made the original Eve gate work.
**Cited in:** [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s)

## Persons

### Anar Abdul

Thukker chief who echoed AMP's concerns over Republic-backed experimentation in liberated Barbican. First cross-empire validation of the Drifter-tech risk warning — and a structural reminder that the Thukker speak independently of the Republic.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI)

### Rio Talifo

AMP founder. Has a character seeded in every wormhole system. Public face of the Drifter-tech risk warning. Vindicated repeatedly by Jita-gate, the prismatic sites, and the Hollow Clone Disaster.
**Cited in:** [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI)

## Organizations

### AIR Laboratories

Ishukone-built research project. Major CONCORD funding shifted to AIR around the same time — a Mountain/Ocean win against Forest. A former Sister of Eve sits on its leadership; ties to SoE wormhole research are structural, not coincidental.

### Anoyus Mapping Project (AMP)

Anoyus Mapping Project. Capsuleer initiative working with the Federation. Continuous monitoring of every system in wormhole space. Founded by Rio Talifo. First public sounding on Drifter-tech risks; vindicated by Jita-gate.

### EDENCOM

Inter-empire defensive coalition. Ships built by Upwell. Drifter wormhole occupier (one of five). Partnered with Upwell on Sentinel-MZ research. Lost CONCORD funding to AIR Labs in the Mountain/Ocean realignment.

### Triglavian Collective

Abyssal humanoid civilization that emerged from Abyssal Deadspace via the Drifter Crisis upheavals. Conquered 27 systems through the Invasion arc to form Pochven. Tied to abyssal-filament technology and a multi-front conflict with the Drifters. Their proxis-class doctrine sits adjacent to Construct-derived tech without using it the same way — making them a third pole next to Drifter and capsuleer civilizations.

Pre-collapse civilization whose remnants seed modern Anoikis. Their collapse coincides with the Yan Jung's — suggesting a common cause that current EVE lore has not surfaced. Talocan structures are the anchor points for modern wormhole-occupier Drifter tech research.

### Yan Jung

One of three pre-collapse civilizations. Yan Jung gravitonic technology is the structural ancestor of every stargate, warp drive, and cyno beacon in modern New Eden — including the Drone Graviton Emitter the rogue drones are now manufacturing in the Catalyst-era event. Their disappearance ~9000 AD remains canonically unexplained.
**Cited in:** [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s)

## Places

### Pochven

Triglavian-controlled region formed from the 27 Invasion-arc conquered systems. Bordered space between Triglavian abyssal territory and standard New Eden. Recent Deathless / Archbine activity has used Pochven as a staging zone, and the Zarzakh-to-Pochven corridor is one of the few non-empire transit routes the Deathless Network exploits.
**Cited in:** [The Scope – One Year Anniversary Of Pochven](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/the-scope-one-year-anniversary-of-pochven) · [The Scope - War in Delve and Formation of Pochven](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/the-scope-war-in-delve-and-formation-of-pochven)

### Tanu II

Capital planet of the Ammatar Mandate. Site of one of the YC123 dread attacks — the canonical precedent for the high-sec cyno tech now being reused for the Syndicate breakout. Currently hosts a memorial bookmark for Empress Catiz's Honor Guard.
**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

## Phenomenons

### The Construct

The Construct is the Jovian deep state — every Jove who matters has spent significant subjective time inside it. The 'one body, one mind' law is the constitutional safeguard around it. The Sleeper civilization is what happens when Jove-bound consciousnesses commit fully to the Construct and never come out. The Drifter emergence is canonically tied to whatever happened to those consciousnesses afterward.
**Cited in:** [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s)

### Drifter Hyperspace Network

Drifter-origin data-transfer / hyperspace infrastructure. Manipulated for the Jita-gate attack. Access only via the five wormhole occupiers — the access list is what forces the Caldari Jita-gate accusation against Gallente outward toward a third-party co-conspirator.

### Highsec Cyno Beacon Technology

Illegal/captured cyno-beacon tech that bypasses empire jamming. Canon since the YC123 dread assaults at Tanu and Junfaa. Re-weaponized for the YC128 Syndicate breakout — the single load-bearing piece of inheritance between the Old-Wars-era and the Warpath arc.

### Hollow Clone Disaster

Cloning failure cascade emerging during Crimson Harvest. Ash's theory: caused by Drifter-tech meddling. Order of St. Tetrimon now performing cleansing burns of failed clones. The biological consequence of the destructive-testing era.

### Jovian Disease

The chronic existential crisis of the Jove. Every Jovian arc — turning to the Construct, the Sleeper transition, eventual Drifter emergence — is a response to this defect. Insorum's structural analog: Vitoxin is to the Mimitar what Jovian Disease is to the Jove, except the Jove engineered theirs themselves.
**Cited in:** [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s) · [Jovian admiral Ouria known to have the Jovian disease](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/jovian-admiral-ouria-known-to-have-the-jovian-disease)

### Prismatic / Fractured Sites

New Catalyst-era sites containing destroyed Drifter-structure wrecks. Wreck inventory matches Pochven-zone Drifter wormhole-generator hardware. Structural confirmation that occupier-faction Drifter testing is producing fallout in known space.
**Cited in:** [Hoboleaks — Warpath Cycle Items](https://sde.hoboleaks.space/)

## Artifacts

### Drone Graviton Emitter

Item from the Catalyst-era drone event. Drops from sentient rogue drones; turn-in for Consortium tractor blueprints. Ash's theory: ties to needle-jack filament technology potentially developed by rogue drones — graviton manipulation is the underlying physics of all Eve spacetime tech.
**Cited in:** [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (recorded YC126.04.04)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI)
