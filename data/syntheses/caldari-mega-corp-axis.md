## Events

### Crielere Project (collapse) *(YC105)*

YC105 Federation–State joint research project announced as a milestone in inter-empire cooperation. Caldari police were investigated after sabotage and vandalism accusations, and the project collapsed amid intra-State factional conflict. Foreshadowed the modern Mountain/Ocean axis: pro-cooperation factions (later Ishukone-aligned) vs anti-Federation factions (later Provist/Heth-aligned).

## Persons

### Akimaka Saraki

CEP Chairman; Mountain Faction. Public face of the Caldari response across the Warpath cycle — pledged 'protection' of Syndicate Intaki against Syndicate leadership itself. Allied with Ocean against Forest in Ash's framework, which makes every action he takes structurally anti-Lai-Dai.

### Tibus Heth *(YC108–YC115)*

The most disruptive Caldari political figure of the modern era. Heth's reclamation of Caldari Prime turned populist anger into a cross-corp alliance — the Provost — that swept aside the post-rebellion mega-corp consensus. Under Heth, Ytiri was absorbed into the alliance (taking Star Seed onto the Ishukone-axis side), Sukuuvestaa pushed back, and the State briefly operated as a single political bloc. His fall ~YC115 left a power vacuum that the Mountain + Ocean factions filled, completing the Lai-Dai-vs-Ishukone realignment Ash now diagrams.

Forest-faction-aligned, making Hyasyoda a Lai Dai-axis sister-corp on Ash's structural read of Caldari politics.
