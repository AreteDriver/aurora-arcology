## Events

### Crielere Project Collapse *(YC105)*

The Caldari–Federation joint research initiative at Crielere was announced in YC105 as a milestone in inter-empire cooperation. [Caldari and Gallente dignitaries attend conference at Crielere - Foiritan causes stir (YC 105)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/caldari-and-gallente-dignitaries-attend-conference-at-crielere-foiritan-causes-stir/) Caldari police were placed under investigation after accusations of sabotage and vandalism, [Caldari police forces in Crielere under investigation after accusations (YC 105)](https://www.eveonline.com/article/caldari-police-forces-in-crielere-are-currently-under-investigation-after-serious-accusations.) and the project ultimately collapsed amid intra-State factional conflict. [Crielere Project announced (YC105)](https://www.eveonline.com/article/the-state-and-federation-open-new-research-center-1) · [Valuable blueprints missing from the Crielere Research facility (YC 106)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/valuable-blueprints-missing-from-the-crielere-research-facility/) Ishukone CEO Gariushi unveiled discoveries from the project before its fall. [Ishukone CEO reveals new technologies from Crielere (YC 105)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/ishukone-ceo-gariushi-unveils-crielere-discoveries/) The episode foreshadowed the modern Mountain/Ocean axis split: pro-cooperation factions later aligned with Ishukone against the anti-Federation factions that became the Provist/Heth alignment.

### Innovaatio Varustukset Scandal *(YC109)*

YC109 saw Caldari military-development corp Innovaatio Varustukset destroyed by an espionage scandal that ended in sale and ruin. [Innovaatio Varustukset scandal ends with sale and ruin (YC 109)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/espionage-scandal-causes-innva-ruin/) The corp's laboratory was robbed [Innovaatio Varustukset laboratory robbed (YC 109)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-varustukset-laboratory-robbed/) and its scientists were kidnapped before being rescued. [Innovaatio Varustukset scientists rescued after kidnapping (YC 109)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-varustukset-scientists-kidnapped-rescued/) A multi-corp bidding war involving Kaalakiota, Wiyrkomi, and Sukuuvestaa preceded the final sale. [Innovaatio sale halted by last minute scandal (YC 109)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/innovaatio-sells-to-ishukone-for-surprising-sum/) The episode prefigured the Heth-era Provist consolidation pattern of state-favored megacorp absorption of failed Caldari enterprises.

### Caldari Constructions Workers' Revolt *(YC110)*

In YC110 a labor revolt broke out at Caldari Constructions facilities, led by foreman-turned-revolutionary Tibus Heth. [Caldari Constructions workers revolt (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/breaking-news-worker-revolt-underway-at-caldari-constructions-facilities-1/) The Mercantile Club hosted discussions on the takeover [Mercantile Club hosts discussion on Caldari Constructions takeover (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/mercantile-club-hosts-discussion-on-caldari-constructions-takeover/) while the Federation cast a wary eye on what was being called "the Heth situation." [Federation casts a wary eye at the 'Heth situation' (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/federation-casts-a-wary-eye-at-the-heth-situation/) This was the structural origin of Heth's rise — predating Malkalen and the Empyrean Age. Without Caldari Constructions, there is no Provist regime. Share prices at the corp rose years later on reports of economic recovery, [Caldari Constructions share prices rise on news of economic recovery (YC 115)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/caldari-constructions-share-prices-rise-on-reports-of-economic-recovery/) and the company subsequently moved to nullify Heth's shares. [Caldari Constructions to nullify Heth's shares (YC 115)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/caldari-constructions-to-nullify-heths-shares/)

### Semiki Biohazard Containment Failure *(YC121)*

In YC121 a biohazard containment failure occurred at Semiki, with Lai Dai coming under suspicion in the contamination cycle. [Semiki Zainou biohazard containment compromised (YC 121)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/semiki-zainou-biohazard-containment-compromised/) The situation deteriorated, prompting Ishukone Watch to release initial findings, [Semiki Situation Deteriorates; Ishukone Watch Releases Initial Findings](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/semiki-situation-deteriorates-ishukone-watch-releases-initial-findings) and Ishukone and the CEP subsequently battled SARO in the CONCORD Assembly over dangerous research materials connected to the incident. [Ishukone and CEP Battle SARO in CONCORD Assembly Over Dangerous Research Materials](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/ishukone-and-cep-battle-saro-in-concord-assembly-over-dangerous-research-materials)

## Persons

CEP Chairman and Mountain Faction representative. Saraki serves as the public face of the Caldari response across the Warpath cycle, having pledged Caldari "protection" of Syndicate Intaki against Syndicate leadership itself. [New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21 - YC128-04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [BREAKING NEWS! CALDARI INVADES THE SYNDICATE!!!! - EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) His Mountain Faction is allied with Ocean against Forest, which makes every action he takes structurally anti-Lai Dai.

### Mens Reppola

Ishukone CEO since Otro Gariushi's death. [Mens Reppola Made Ishukone CEO, Charts Future Course](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/mens-reppola-made-ishukone-ceo-charts-future-course) The structural anchor of the Ishukone axis. Federation policymakers exhibited muted reactions to his appointment. [Federation Policymakers Exhibit Muted Reactions to Mens Reppola Appointment](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/federation-policymakers-exhibit-muted-reactions-to-mens-reppola-appointment) He is working with Mordu's Legion (Muryia Mordu) on clone-soldier development, [EVE: Templar One (Tony Gonzales)](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/templar-one) · [Ante (Chronicle)](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante) and was operating under heavy drone protection and in semi-hiding by the end of the Templar One period. He also announced a personal five-percent stake in Ishukone. [Mens Reppola Announces 5% Stake In Ishukone](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/mens-reppola-announces-5-stake-in-ishukone) Every major Ishukone-axis development in the YC128 cycle routes back through him.

Former Ishukone CEO. A Guristas-aligned pilot, Gariushi rose to power by exposing the corruption of his predecessor Akelan through the Raven project — Miller and Riley Rashemo helped engineer the takeover. [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (recorded YC126.04.04)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) He defended a deal with the Khanid Kingdom on transcranial microcontrollers, claiming the chips improved slaves' lives. [Otro Gariushi defends Dark Amarr deal - claims chips improve slaves' lives (YC 106)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/otro-gariushi-defends-dark-amarr-deal-claims-chips-improve-slaves-lives-1/) He was killed at the YC110 Caldari–Federation summit when Alexander Noir's Nyx struck the station. [ISHUKONE STATEMENT: OTRO GARIUSHI CONFIRMED DEAD](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/ishukone-statement-otro-gariushi-confirmed-dead) · [EVE: The Empyrean Age (Tony Gonzales)](https://www.eveonline.com/news/view/the-empyrean-age) The "broker" responsible had specifically targeted him because Ishukone was developing Insorum, the Vitoxin antidote.

Founder of Outer Ring Excavations (YC91). [Outer Ring Excavations founded (YC 91)](http://wiki.eve-inspiracy.com/index.php?title=Yani_Sar_Arteu) In the aftermath of the YC117 Citadel-era station seizures, Arteu personally confirmed ORE's membership in the Upwell Consortium — a canonical statement that locked the Upwell–megacorp alignment graph. [ORE membership of Upwell Consortium confirmed by Yani Sar Arteu in aftermath of station seizures (YC 117)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/ore-membership-of-upwell-consortium-confirmed-by-yani-sar-arteu-in-aftermath-of-station-seizures/) · [ORE Membership of Upwell Consortium Confirmed by Yani Sar Arteu in Aftermath of Station Seizures](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/ore-membership-of-upwell-consortium-confirmed-by-yani-sar-arteu-in-aftermath-of-station-seizures) A reclusive industrial figure, Arteu has minimal political exposure outside ORE business.

### Tibus Heth *(YC108–YC115)*

The most disruptive Caldari political figure of the modern era. A foreman-turned-revolutionary, Heth's reclamation of Caldari Prime converted populist anger into the cross-corp Provist alliance that swept aside the post-rebellion megacorp consensus. [BREAKING NEWS: TIBUS HETH CONQUERS CALDARI PRIME, FOIRITAN CEDES CONTROL](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-tibus-heth-conquers-caldari-prime-foiritan-cedes-control) Under Heth, Ytiri was absorbed into the Provist alliance — taking Star Seed onto the Ishukone axis — while Sukuuvestaa pushed back and the State briefly operated as a single political bloc. He created a State Loyalty Tribunal [Tibus Heth Creates State Loyalty Tribunal](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/tibus-heth-creates-state-loyalty-tribunal) and was later shot by an unknown assailant. [TIBUS HETH SHOT BY MYSTERY ASSAILANT AT NEW CALDARI POLITICAL RALLY, CONDITION UNKNOWN](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/tibus-heth-shot-by-mystery-assailant-at-new-caldari-political-rally-condition-unknown) He was subsequently denounced by the CEP and removed as KK CEO [BREAKING NEWS: Tibus Heth denounced by CEP, to be ousted as KK CEO](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-tibus-heth-denounced-by-cep-to-be-ousted-as-kk-ceo) after declaring a plan to nationalize all megacorps. [Tibus Heth declares plan to nationalize megacorps; KK revealed to be in financial straits](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/tibus-heth-declares-plan-to-nationalize-megacorps-kk-revealed-to-be-in-financial-straits) Provists seized Haatomo station with Heth leading [Breaking News: Provists seize Haatamo station; Tibus Heth leading](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-provists-seize-haatamo-station-tibus-heth-leading) before the Caldari Navy stormed the facility. [Caldari Navy storms Haatomo station; location of Heth unknown](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/caldari-navy-storms-haatomo-station-location-of-heth-unknown) His fall around YC115 left a power vacuum that the Mountain and Ocean factions filled, completing the Lai Dai-vs-Ishukone realignment that structures current Caldari politics.

The Caldari State's unified military fleet. Operationally controlled by the Caldari Executive Panel through the authority chain running from the Provost era through Saraki's current chairmanship, it has been the backbone of every major State military operation since the Empyrean Age — including the YC110 invasion of Caldari Prime, [BREAKING NEWS: CALDARI NAVY BOMBARDS CALDARI PRIME, DROPSHIPS LAND ON SURFACE](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-caldari-navy-bombards-caldari-prime-dropships-land-on-surface) the YC115 capitulation theater, and the YC128 Syndicate conquest. [Caldari State Conquers Syndicate, Breakout Expected](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/caldari-state-conquers-syndicate-breakout-expected) The Navy publicly acknowledged the efficacy of cloned soldiers [Caldari Navy publicly acknowledges efficacy of cloned soldiers](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/caldari-navy-publicly-acknowledges-efficacy-of-cloned-soldiers) and, alongside the CEP, greenlighted the Jita 4-4 expansion. [CEP and Caldari Navy Greenlight Jita 4-4 Expansion; PKN Interstellar Consortium Seals Contracts](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/cep-and-caldari-navy-greenlight-jita-4-4-expansion-pkn-interstellar-consortium-seals-contracts) Kaalakiota and Sukuuvestaa provided support to the Navy on military cloning efforts. ### Chief Executive Panel

The Caldari governing body composed of the eight megacorp CEOs, currently chaired by Saraki. A closed-door CEP session following the Jita-gate incident produced the posture that drove the Syndicate conquest
