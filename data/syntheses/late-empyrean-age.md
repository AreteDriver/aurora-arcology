# Late Empyrean Age (YC122–128)

> The current cycle. Triglavian Invasion aftermath, Drifter Crisis maturity, Catiz reorganization, Moreau election, the YC128 Warpath cycle, Saraki consolidation. The era from Pochven formation to Caldari conquest of Syndicate.

*Source-grounded synthesis. Curator-authored claim text, deduplicated. Every citation traces to a primary source.*

### Fall of Niarja *(YC122)*

YC122 Triglavian conquest of Niarja during the Triglavian Invasion — severing the high-sec trade corridor between Amarr and Caldari space and collapsing what was historically the most heavily-trafficked sub-cluster route. The Niarja severance is the structural cause of the Amarr-Caldari trade reorientation that Catiz I's territorial reorganization later codified.

### Triglavian Invasion *(YC122)*

YC122 Triglavian conquest of 27 systems via the abyssal-filament invasion mechanic. Formed Pochven, opened the Triglavian-Drifter conflict to capsuleer participation, and exposed the Niarja trade-route severance that broke the Amarr-Caldari high-sec corridor. EDENCOM's formation as an inter-empire defensive coalition came directly from the Invasion response. Structurally a third-power eruption between the four-empire system and Drifter forces.

### Jita Gate Incident *(2026-04-21)*

The triggering catastrophe. Hyperspace fractures around all seven gates killed thousands and turned Jita into a politically untenable hub for the Caldari State. Crucially, the mechanism — manipulation of Drifter Hyperspace Network data layers — is only accessible to the five wormhole occupiers. That technical fact contradicts the Caldari accusation against the Gallente and forces the conspiracy outward toward Amarr / EDENCOM / Triglavian co-conspirators.

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(2026-04-30)*

The structural fracture of the YC128 cycle. CONCORD's war-response automation collapsed under a precise cyberattack, allowing all four empires to invade each other's high-sec for the first time since the Yulai event of YC110. Marks the moment the high-sec social contract stopped being a structural guarantee and became a narrative one. Origin remains unattributed; the message text reads as Guristas-flavored.

## Places

### Pochven

Triglavian-controlled region formed from the 27 Invasion-arc conquered systems. Bordered space between Triglavian abyssal territory and standard New Eden. Recent Deathless / Archbine activity has used Pochven as a staging zone, and the Zarzakh-to-Pochven corridor is one of the few non-empire transit routes the Deathless Network exploits.

### Syndicate Region

Formerly NPC-nullsec. Caldari sovereign post-YC128.05.05. Strategic value: only NPC-nullsec adjacent to Gallente highsec via PFTAC. Annexation severs Federation contiguity unless Aguard's stargate holds.

### Zarzakh

The Deathless's home base. A hidden system with EVE-gate-adjacent properties revealed during the Equinox-era Deathless arc. The Deathless Network's coordination hub — pirate factions report to Zarzakh, Archbine defectors transit through it, and the Shikari operations are run from it. Sits structurally where Pochven meets the Eve Gate periphery.
