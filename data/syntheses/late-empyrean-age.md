### Fall of Niarja *(YC122)*

The Triglavian conquest of Niarja severed the high-sec trade corridor between Amarr and Caldari space, collapsing what had historically been the most heavily-trafficked sub-cluster route. The Niarja severance is the structural cause of the Amarr-Caldari trade reorientation that Catiz I's territorial reorganization later codified.

### Triglavian Invasion *(YC122)*

The Triglavian conquest of 27 systems via the abyssal-filament invasion mechanic formed Pochven, opened the Triglavian-Drifter conflict to capsuleer participation, and exposed the Niarja trade-route severance that broke the Amarr-Caldari high-sec corridor. EDENCOM's formation as an inter-empire defensive coalition came directly from the invasion response. The event is structurally a third-power eruption between the four-empire system and Drifter forces.

### Gallente Presidential Election *(YC128-04-17)*

Alix Moreau of the Autonomist Party won with 34.8%, narrowly beating Progressive candidate Soraya Roden, with Lucas Tenzin placing third — one of the closest elections on record. Moreau's narrow win over Roden inverted the expected hawkish response to Caldari escalation: the Federation elected a reformer at the exact moment State aggression peaked, structurally weakening the Federation military response and giving Mountain and Ocean factions an opening they would not have had under a Roden presidency.

### Jita Gate Incident *(YC128-04-21)*

All seven stargates into Jita were damaged simultaneously by hyperspace fractures matching those seen in fractured fields. The death toll ran into the thousands, and ongoing radiation remained visible from nearby systems. The mechanism was manipulation of the Drifter Hyperspace Network data-transfer layer — infrastructure accessible only to the five wormhole occupiers. That technical fact contradicts the Caldari accusation against the Gallente and pushes the conspiracy outward toward Amarr, EDENCOM, or Triglavian co-conspirators.

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(YC128-04-30)*

CONCORD's war-response system was disabled by a cyberattack of unprecedented complexity. Within minutes, all four empires had launched cross-incursions into each other's high-sec — the first such event since the Yulai incident of YC110. A single message was left behind: *'Warpath, a gift dear empires. After all, toys are meant to be played with.'* Origin remains unattributed. The failure marks the moment the high-sec social contract stopped being a structural guarantee and became a narrative one.

Silphy en Diabel was detained in New Caldari by Lai Dai Protection Services after two Syndicate agents convicted in March named her under a plea agreement. Without this arrest, the conquest framing that followed would not have held diplomatically: her detention gave the Caldari Navy the legal cover to frame the broader Syndicate as compromised.

### Caldari Conquest of Syndicate *(YC128-05-05)*

The Caldari Navy declared sovereignty over all 106 Syndicate systems. Most local authorities surrendered with limited fire exchanged, making this the first forcible empire annexation of nullsec in modern New Eden history. The action was framed as retaliation for Silphy's alleged role in Jita-gate, and strategically eliminated the buffer state that Lai Dai had previously operated through.
