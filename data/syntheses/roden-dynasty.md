## Events

### Federation Day *(YC0+)*

The annual Gallente Federation founding holiday serves as a cultural anchor of Federation identity. Presidential addresses traditionally fall within the Federation Day cycle, and the holiday functions as a structural counterpart to the Caldari Liberation Day and Republic Liberation Day observances.

**Cited in:** [Federation celebrates Federation Day (YC109)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/gallente-federation-celebrates-federation-day/) · [Federation day (YC114)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/federation-day-yc114/) · [Galactic Hour News Roundup: Gallente Federation Day (YC122)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/galactic-hour-news-roundup-gallente-federation-day-yc122-edition/)

### Federation Seizure of Intaki *(YC124)*

The Federation aggressively removed Intaki and two adjacent constellations from the Faction Warfare war zone and made them full Federation property. This triggered the Uprising arc and the original Caldari–Intaki Syndicate constellation deal — the agreement that the subsequent Caldari conquest later invalidated.

**Cited in:** [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (YC126)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI)

### Gallente Presidential Election YC128 *(YC128)*

Alix Moreau of the Autonomist Party won 34.8% of the vote, narrowly defeating Soraya Roden of the Progressive Party, with Lucas Tenzin placing third. The result was one of the closest elections on record. Moreau's narrow win over Roden inverted the expected hawkish Federation response to Caldari escalation — the Federation elected a reformer at the precise moment State aggression peaked.

## Organizations

### Roden Shipyards

Federation shipbuilder granted extensive Federation construction contracts in YC105, with competitors protesting the awards. In YC107 the corporation produced the first privately owned Gallente Navy-spec vessel. The Roden family's commercial success from these contracts underwrote Soraya Roden's YC128 presidential candidacy.

### Federal Defense Union

The Gallente Federation's Faction Warfare militia — the volunteer and capsuleer-pilot frontline against the State in the FW war zone. The FDU operationally executed the YC124 Intaki seizure and operates under Federation Navy authority.

**Cited in:** [First Federal Defense Union carrier killed (YC110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/first-federal-defense-union-carrier-killed/)

### Federation Navy

The Gallente Federation's military fleet. Conducted the YC124 invasion of Intaki and the adjacent constellations that triggered the Uprising arc and the subsequent constellation deal with the Intaki Syndicate — the deal that the YC128 conquest has since invalidated. Currently under President Moreau's authority; hawkish Senate factions have expressed concern that her Autonomist-Party origins make it difficult for her to direct it firmly.
