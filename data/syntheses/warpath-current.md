## Events

### Zephyr Ship Distribution *(YC111)*

In YC111, Polymath Valus en Diabel authorized the Intaki Syndicate to mass-distribute the Zephyr exploration ship to all registered capsuleers. Sleeper-ignored by design, the Zephyr allowed capsuleers to map wormhole space — and quietly funneled telemetry data back through the Syndicate to Ishukone. It is the original instance of the Syndicate operating as a data-conduit, structurally identical to the information flows that later sit inside Upwell. ([Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Ante (Chronicle)](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante))

### Capsuleer Day *(YC117+)*

Upwell Consortium established the annual capsuleer-recognition holiday in YC117. Capsuleer Day XXIII (YC128) falls during the Warpath Protocol failure cycle — the first such holiday observed during a CONCORD-system breach since the YC110 Yulai gate event. ([Upwell Consortium announces capsuleer day (YC118)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/upwell-consortium-announces-capsuleer-day/) · [Galactic Hour News Roundup: Capsuleer Day (YC121)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/galactic-hour-news-roundup-capsuleer-day-yc121-edition/))

### YC124 Caldari–Syndicate Protection Pact *(YC124)*

The Caldari State signed a protection agreement with the Intaki Syndicate in exchange for control of a border constellation (DSTC-M4Q). The pact reflected the historically sympathetic State–Syndicate relationship and was dissolved once the State attributed the YC128 Jita-gate attacks to the Syndicate. ([EVE Universe Show YC128.05.07](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oNGS34eUCmM) · [The Scope — Intaki Syndicate Fleet Attempts Breakout](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfqboHJUNpw))

### Gallente Presidential Election YC128 *(YC128.04.17)*

Alix Moreau of the Autonomist Party won the Federation presidency with 34.8% of the vote, narrowly defeating Soraya Roden of the Progressive Party. Lucas Tenzin placed third. The election was one of the closest on record. ### Jita Gate Incident *(YC128.04.21)*

All seven stargates into Jita were simultaneously damaged by hyperspace fractures matching those seen in fractured fields, killing thousands. The mechanism involved manipulation of the Drifter Hyperspace Network data-transfer layer — accessible only to the five wormhole occupiers. Ongoing radiation remained visible from nearby systems. ([New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21–04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) · [Gates into Jita Damaged As Tensions Between Empires Rise](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/gates-into-jita-damaged-as-tensions-between-empires-rise))

### Silphy Summoned to New Caldari *(YC128.04.28)*

Three days before her arrest, Intaki Syndicate Chair Silphy en Diabel was summoned to New Caldari for a State inquest into Syndicate involvement in the gate disaster. The summons signaled the collapse of the Syndicate–Caldari constellation deal and gave the State the procedural pretext to detain a foreign head of state. ([New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21–04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [Jita Citizens Protest State Response, CONCORD to Negotiate](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/jita-citizens-protest-state-response-concord-to-negotiate))

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(YC128.04.30)*

CONCORD's war-response system was disabled by a cyberattack of unprecedented complexity. Within minutes, all four empires launched cross-incursions into each other's high-sec — the first such event since Yulai YC110. A single message was left on impacted CONCORD terminals: *'Warpath, a gift dear empires. After all, toys are meant to be played with.'* The origin of the attack remained unattributed. ([New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21–04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) · [Warpath Protocol Failure Brings War To High-Sec](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/warpath-protocol-failure-brings-war-to-high-sec))

### Arrest of Silphy en Diabel *(YC128.05.01)*

Silphy en Diabel was detained in New Caldari by Lai Dai Protection Services. Two Syndicate agents convicted in March had named her under a plea agreement, giving the Caldari Navy the legal cover to detain her and frame the broader Syndicate as compromised. ### Caldari Conquest of Syndicate *(YC128.05.05)*

The Caldari Navy declared sovereignty over all 106 Syndicate systems. Most local authorities surrendered with limited exchange of fire. It was the first forcible empire annexation of nullsec in modern New Eden history, framed as retaliation for Silphy's role in the Jita-gate incident. Executive General Haikoriggo announced the operation from the Intaki Syndicate Bureau on Poitot. ([EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0) · [Caldari State Conquers Syndicate, Breakout Expected](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/caldari-state-conquers-syndicate-breakout-expected) · [The SCOPE — Syndicate Breakout Attempt Ends in Destruction](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/the-scope-syndicate-breakout-attempt-ends-in))

### Orvolle Breakout Interception and Syndicate Capital Breakout *(YC128.05.07)*

Three escort-intercept windows opened in the highsec constellations of Pelille, Bayet, and Orvolle. The first confirmed attempt saw a Syndicate fleet — three Moros-class dreadnoughts, two Nyx-class supercarriers, and one Erebus-class Titan — jump to an unregistered mobile cynosural beacon in high-sec Orvolle. The fleet was flagged CONCORD-suspect and destroyed by capsuleer loyalists; capital-grade weapons proved ineffective against sub-capitals in high-sec. The Scope's Alton Haveri anchored live coverage from Orvolle. Caldari Navy Executive General Heika Torigo had pre-warned loyalist capsuleers that multiple Syndicate breakout attempts were anticipated over the following two days, and directed the post-conquest bounty program against Syndicate vessels. The Syndicate fleets used captured high-sec cyno-beacon technology inherited from the YC123 dread assaults. ([EVE Universe Show YC128.05.07](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oNGS34eUCmM) · [The SCOPE — Syndicate Breakout Attempt Ends in Destruction](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/the-scope-syndicate-breakout-attempt-ends-in) · [The Scope — Intaki Syndicate Fleet Attempts Breakout](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfqboHJUNpw))

## People

### Akimaka Saraki

CEP Chairman; Mountain Faction. Public face of the Caldari response across the Warpath cycle — he pledged Caldari 'protection' of Syndicate Intaki against Syndicate leadership itself, and allied with the Ocean faction against Forest. ([New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21–04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk) · [EVE Universe Show YC128.05.05](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7g-FhxK4vJ0))

### Alexander Ducasse

Evermore President. Mediated the Caldari–Jita-resident negotiations and controls the IMN media network. The Innerbus-to-Evermore restructuring that placed him in that role reads as a planned takeover targeting Amarr partnerships. ([New Eden News: Warpath YC128-04-21–04-30](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QUoUNxfSXUk))

### Alix Moreau

New Gallente Federation President, Autonomist Party. Inaugurated under the Jita-gate cloud, she issued amnesty to Syndicate descendants during the conquest and positioned the Federation against Caldari rule. Hawkish Senate factions have expressed concern over her military inexperience.
