# Warpath / Syndicate Conquest (current)

> The YC128.04–05 cycle: Jita-gate, Warpath Protocol failure, Silphy arrest, Caldari conquest of Syndicate, capital breakout. Includes the Silphy-chronicle backstory thread (SoE / Antagonists / Mens-data-trade) that ties her arrest to the deeper structure.

*Source-grounded synthesis. Curator-authored claim text, deduplicated. Every citation traces to a primary source.*

## Events

### Zephyr Ship Distribution (Apocrypha) *(YC111)*

The YC111 mass distribution of the Zephyr exploration ship by the Intaki Syndicate. Sleeper-ignored, it allowed capsuleers to map wormhole space — and quietly funneled telemetry data back through the Syndicate to Ishukone. The Zephyr is the original instance of Syndicate-as-data-conduit, structurally identical to the data flows that later sit inside Upwell.
**Cited in:** [Silphy - The Faction Leader You Never Hear About (recorded YC126.04.04)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWvC8dlYBjI) · [Ante (Chronicle)](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/ante)

### Gallente Presidential Election YC128 *(2026-04-17)*

Moreau's narrow win over Roden inverted the expected hawkish response to Caldari escalation. The Federation elected a reformer at the exact moment State aggression peaked, structurally weakening the Federation military response and giving Mountain/Ocean an opening they wouldn't have had under a Roden presidency.

### Jita Gate Incident *(2026-04-21)*

The triggering catastrophe. Hyperspace fractures around all seven gates killed thousands and turned Jita into a politically untenable hub for the Caldari State. Crucially, the mechanism — manipulation of Drifter Hyperspace Network data layers — is only accessible to the five wormhole occupiers. That technical fact contradicts the Caldari accusation against the Gallente and forces the conspiracy outward toward Amarr / EDENCOM / Triglavian co-conspirators.

### Silphy Summoned to New Caldari *(2026-04-28)*

The diplomatic on-ramp to Silphy en Diabel's arrest. Three days before her detention, the Caldari summoned her to New Caldari for inquest. The summons signaled that the Syndicate–Caldari constellation deal had collapsed and gave the State the procedural pretext to detain a foreign head of state.

### Warpath Protocol Failure *(2026-04-30)*

The structural fracture of the YC128 cycle. CONCORD's war-response automation collapsed under a precise cyberattack, allowing all four empires to invade each other's high-sec for the first time since the Yulai event of YC110. Marks the moment the high-sec social contract stopped being a structural guarantee and became a narrative one. Origin remains unattributed; the message text reads as Guristas-flavored.

### Arrest of Silphy en Diabel *(2026-05-01)*

The pivot point between Jita-gate and the Syndicate conquest. Two Syndicate agents convicted in March named Silphy under plea, giving the Caldari Navy the legal cover to detain her in New Caldari and frame the broader Syndicate as compromised. Without this arrest, the conquest framing would not have held diplomatically.

### Caldari Conquest of Syndicate *(2026-05-05)*

The first forcible empire annexation of nullsec in modern New Eden history. Caldari Navy seized 106 systems against minimal resistance, framed as retaliation for Silphy's role in Jita-gate. Strategically eliminates the buffer state Lai Dai had been working through; structurally hands Mountain+Ocean factions the win they couldn't get politically.

### Syndicate Capital Breakout *(2026-05-07)*

The capsuleer-facing climax of the conquest. Three highsec constellation windows allow the Syndicate fugitive capitals — using captured high-sec cyno tech inherited from the YC123 dread assaults — to attempt escape. CCP-built escort intercept event, but lore-significant because the same tech links forward to next month's expansion.

### Akimaka Saraki

CEP Chairman; Mountain Faction. Public face of the Caldari response across the Warpath cycle — pledged 'protection' of Syndicate Intaki against Syndicate leadership itself. Allied with Ocean against Forest in Ash's framework, which makes every action he takes structurally anti-Lai-Dai.

Evermore President. Mediator in the Caldari–Jita-resident negotiations. Controls IMN media. ## Places

### Jita

New Eden's primary trade hub. All seven gates damaged YC128.04.21. Resident-identity survey: most identify as 'Jita first, Caldari second' — atypical within the State. Structurally too important to fail; structurally now political dynamite.

### Lonetrek region

Caldari State core region. Contains Jita (the trade hub) and the major State industrial zones. Operational center for State Protectorate FW militia operations and the State Workers' Union activity. The Jita-gate incident occurred at Lonetrek's primary trade artery.

### Poitot

Syndicate's de-facto capital. Only named Syndicate system. Site of Silphy's seat — Haikoriggo announced the operation from the Intaki Syndicate Bureau here. The symbolic and operational center of the conquest.

### Syndicate Region

Formerly NPC-nullsec. Caldari sovereign post-YC128.05.05. Strategic value: only NPC-nullsec adjacent to Gallente highsec via PFTAC. Annexation severs Federation contiguity unless Aguard's stargate holds.

### Thera

The Sisters of EVE's deep-research base, hidden in Anoikis until externally discovered. Sentona Sarpati's organization runs its non-humanitarian work from here. Structurally adjacent to every Drifter / Sleeper / hyperspace-network thread — the SoE-AIR-EDENCOM research pipeline runs through this site. CONCORD's headquarters system. Site of the Yulai Accords (the inter-empire governance treaty). Symbolic center of inter-empire governance — and the YC110 Yulai gate breach is the only prior precedent for the YC128 Warpath Protocol failure. Capsuleer SoCT briefings happen here.

## Phenomenons

### Drifter Hyperspace Network

Drifter-origin data-transfer / hyperspace infrastructure. Manipulated for the Jita-gate attack. Access only via the five wormhole occupiers — the access list is what forces the Caldari Jita-gate accusation against Gallente outward toward a third-party co-conspirator.

### Highsec Cyno Beacon Technology

Illegal/captured cyno-beacon tech that bypasses empire jamming. Canon since the YC123 dread assaults at Tanu and Junfaa. Re-weaponized for the YC128 Syndicate breakout — the single load-bearing piece of inheritance between the Old-Wars-era and the Warpath arc.
