## Events

### Amarr Reclaiming

The Amarr Empire's pre-history expansion cycle built its dominion through the religious-imperial subjugation of neighboring peoples. The Minmatar conquest stands as its climactic chapter; the Khanid secession its first major failure; the Battle of Vak'Atioth, when the Jove repulsed Amarr expansion, its eventual ceiling.

### Salvation Crusade *(YC111)*

In YC111, Minmatar preacher Jarek launched a trans-empire religious mobilization that crossed the Amarr–Republic border. The Crusade encountered hostile ships in Heimatar, with a firefight averted by mercenary intervention. Gallente medical charities came under pressure for supporting the Crusade, and a camp on Matar was stormed by rioters with its chapel burned to the ground. Jarek later admitted a "sin of pride" as the Crusade gained converts, and died in an attack on a Salvation Crusade township.

### Lost Adama Discovery, Pure Faith Harvest Raid, and Kahah Chemical Attacks *(YC120)*

In YC120, Ida monk Negui Erlon claimed that an Intaki slave named Eloa Enaro, held aboard the Khanid cruiser Pure Faith Harvest, was a reincarnated Adama who had vanished in YC99. Khanid Sebiros Atsa Kufair refused to sell or surrender her. The dispute escalated when Intaki extremists assaulted the cruiser near a stargate at Vasala; the Royal Khanid Navy repulsed the raid. A Federation–Republic sanctions wave against the Empire followed. ([Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo))

Later that same year, a coordinated weaponized strike combining Death Glow and Insorum struck across the Kahah system, triggering slave revolts. The Khanid pacification that followed, commanded by Allar Chakaide of the 19th Royal Uolans, killed somewhere between 300,000 and 9 million people by varying estimates. Chakaide openly accused the Sisters of Eve and the Mimitar of orchestrating the chemical attacks; the Mimitar countered that Chakaide himself had Blood Raider ties. Neither claim has been resolved canonically. ([Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)) · [Major Chemical Weapons Attacks in Khanid Kingdom; Queen Zidarez Missing; Slave Revolts Reported](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/major-chemical-weapons-attacks-in-khanid-kingdom-queen-zidarez-missing-slave-revolts-reported)

## People

### Negui Erlon

Ida monk who, in YC120, claimed to discover the Lost Adama aboard a Khanid cruiser — identifying Intaki slave Eloa Enaro as a reincarnated Adama who had vanished in YC99. His claim turned a slave purchase dispute into a religious-extremist flashpoint.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Eloa Enaro

Intaki slave aboard the Pure Faith Harvest, claimed by the Ida faith to be a reincarnated lost Adama from YC99.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Atsa Kufair

Khanid Sebiros and owner of the Pure Faith Harvest. Refused to sell or surrender Eloa Enaro despite the Ida monk's claims, making him the proximate cause of the Pure Faith Harvest raid.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Allar Chakaide

Khanid Sebiros, Plenipotentiary, and commander of the 19th Royal Uolans. Led the brutal YC120 Kahah pacification. Openly hostile to the Ammatar; accused the Sisters of Eve and the Mimitar of orchestrating the chemical attacks. The Mimitar countered that he had Blood Raider ties — neither claim has been resolved canonically.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Kantar Yun

Mimitar ambassador to CONCORD. His public hypocrisy speech triggered the chain that led the Mimitar Elders to reveal the secret Elder Fleet project to him.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

## Organizations

### 19th Royal Uolans

Khanid regiment commanded by Allar Chakaide. Carried out the YC120 Kahah pacification; the clone facility holding Chakaide's own clone was a target of the viral strike that started the cycle.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Provost-Marshal Office (Khanid Kingdom)

The Khanid Kingdom's military-judicial office, combining military command authority with imperial-judicial powers in Khanid space. Allar Chakaide held the role during the YC120 Kahah pacification.

### Royal Khanid Navy

The Khanid Kingdom's autonomous military fleet, separate from the Imperial Navy. Repulsed the Pure Faith Harvest raid in YC120 and conducted the Kahah pacification under Allar Chakaide. The 19th Royal Uolans is one of its elite units.

**Cited in:** [Royal Khanid Navy releases statement on Blood Raider attacks](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/royal-khanid-navy-releases-statement-on-blood-raider-attacks)

### Ida Faith / Ida Monastery

Intaki religion. Believes in reincarnation; Adamas reincarnate without technology. The faith's conflict with Khanid slavers over Lost Adama Eloa Enaro radicalized Intaki extremists in YC120.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Blood Raider Covenant

Heretical Amarr blood-cult faction, tied to early Insorum chemical attacks pre-YC120 and to Death Glow harvesting. Allar Chakaide accused them and the Ammatar of conspiring against the Khanid; the Mimitar accused him in return.

**Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo) · [The Blood Raiders](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/the-blood-raiders) · [Blood Raider Covenant Calls on Sani Sabik Cultists to "Rise Up" and Raid Military Cloning Facilities](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/blood-raider-covenant-calls-on-sani-sabik-cultists-to-rise-up-and-raid-military-cloning-facilities) · ["Insorum Imbroglio" Puts Focus on Capsuleers as Eyes Turn Toward Blood Raider Covenant](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/insorum-imbroglio-puts-focus-on-capsuleers-as-eyes-turn-toward-blood-raider-covenant)

### Sani Sabik

Heretical Amarr blood-cult sect and the theological foundation of the Blood Raider Covenant. Believes in blood as sacrament.

### Intaki Syndicate

Loose autonomous federation in the Syndicate region, founded by Caldari-sympathetic Intaki exiles after the Caldari Rebellion. Each station is autonomous; the Syndicate is officially non-political.

**Cited in:** [Silphy en Diabel Arrested in Relation to Jita-gate Incident — New Eden News](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLp1gEBqEw) · [The Scope — Intaki Syndicate Fleet Attempts Breakout](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfqboHJUNpw)

### Ishukone Corporation

Caldari mega-corp, Mountain/Ocean-axis anchor. Original developer of subcranial nano controllers. Acquired Intaki development rights in YC111. Co-developed Insorum (the Vitoxin antidote) — the act that put a target on Otro Gariushi's back. Currently operating a clone-soldier program through Mens Reppola.
