## Events

### Amarr Reclaiming

The religious-imperial expansion that built the Amarr Empire through the subjugation of neighboring peoples. The Minmatar conquest is its climactic chapter; the Khanid secession is its first major failure; the Battle of Vak'Atioth was its eventual ceiling.

### Battle of Vak'Atioth

The Jove Empire annihilated an Amarr Reclaiming fleet and halted the Empire's expansion at the Jove border, establishing the modern Jove-Amarr cold peace and locking in the four-empire balance.

### Caldari Rebellion

The Caldari secession from the Gallente Federation, fought over the Federation's culturally-Gallente integration policy. The Caldari Prime homeworld was lost during the war. The secession founded the modern four-empire system.

### Day of Darkness

The Amarr first-contact-and-conquest of Matar — mythologized as a literal darkening of the sun. Canonically, the Amarr destroyed Matari space infrastructure and began centuries of slavery. Origin point for every downstream Minmatar–Amarr conflict. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo) · [The Ammatars](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/the-ammatars) · [The Day of Darkness](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/the-day-of-darkness)

### Bombardment of Starkmanir Prime / Mass Driver Strike

Idonis Ardishapur ordered sustained Tachyon orbital bombardment of Starkmanir Prime in retaliation for the killing of Archon Ardishapur — the effective Starkmanir genocide. The tribe was believed annihilated for centuries, and the event lit a 250-year fuse to the Great Rebellion. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo) · [Lost Stars](https://universe.eveonline.com/chronicles/lost-stars)

### Mimitar Great Rebellion

The 250-year-delayed answer to the Starkmanir genocide. A Mimitar slave's seizure of an Amarr Khoumak became the rebellion's iconic moment; Gallente assistance enabled the formation of the Mimitar Republic. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

### Aidonis Award to Heideran VII *(YC104)*

The YC104 Aidonis peace-prize award to Heideran VII — a controversial Federation honor for an Amarr Emperor whose Empire still practiced slavery. The award sparked a cycle of murder and mayhem that exposed Empire–Federation civilizational tensions. **Cited in:** [Heideran gets the Aidonis (YC 104)](http://wiki.eve-inspiracy.com/index.php?title=Heideran_Gets_the_Aidonis_\(Chronicle\)) · [Admiral Alexander Noir receives the Aidonis (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/admiral-alexander-noir-receives-the-aidonis/) · [Food supplies to Republic 'at risk' according to Aidonis Medical (YC 111)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/food-supplies-to-republic-at-risk-according-to-aidonis-medical/)

### Minmatar Elder War *(YC110)*

The reappearance of the Minmatar Elder Fleet from Anoikis after centuries of exile. The operation triggered Republic invasions of the Ammatar Mandate and Amarr Empire, the Empress Jamyl I crisis, and the Sansha capital fleet incident in Jove space. **Cited in:** [BREAKING NEWS: IMPERIAL NAVY EMERGES IN KOR-AZOR REGION WITH JAMYL SARUM, ELDER FLEETS RETREATING](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/breaking-news-imperial-navy-emerges-in-kor-azor-region-with-jamyl-sarum-elder-fleets-retreating)

### Minmatar Elders Invasion of Amarr Empire *(YC110)*

The Minmatar Elder-led invasion deep into Amarr space, including the Sarum Prime strike and the Mekhios theater. The Elder Fleet's eventual retreat in Kor-Azor region marked the end of the Republic's deep-strike capability and led to Jamyl's superweapon deployment, which cemented her reign. **Cited in:** [Minmatar Elders launch invasion of Amarr Empire (YC 110)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/report-minmatar-thukker-armada-invades-ammatar-mandate/)

### Sarum Prime Superweapon Strike *(YC110)*

Empress Jamyl I deployed her then-secret superweapon to destroy one Elder Fleet strike group at Sarum Prime. The other two strike groups simultaneously rescued Starkmanir and defecting Nefantar from the Mandate. **Cited in:** [Lore Tourism - The Redemption of the Ammatar](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nQUXg9vhPmo)

A catastrophic plasma-fire event at Seyllin I that killed everyone on the planet, triggering the Apocrypha-era wormhole opening and the discovery of Anoikis. The event awoke Sleeper-civilization remnants and started the chain of Drifter exposure that followed. **Cited in:** [Seyllin Disaster as wormholes emerge (YC 111)](https://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/eve-online-news/weather-satellite-glitch-causes-travel-disruption-1/) · [Minmatar Search For Thukker Caravan; Unable to Respond Significantly to Seyllin Disaster](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/minmatar-search-for-thukker-caravan-unable-to-respond-significantly-to-seyllin-disaster) · [The Fourth Seyllin Conference](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/the-fourth-seyllin-conference)

### Apocrypha-era Wormhole Opening *(YC111)*

The system-wide wormhole opening triggered by the Seyllin Disaster, which exposed Anoikis to capsuleer access for the first time and awoke Sleeper-civilization remnants.
