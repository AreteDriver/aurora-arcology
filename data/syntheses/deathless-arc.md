## Events

### Jovian Admiral Ouria Defection *(YC105)*

In YC105, Jovian Admiral Ouria went rogue — a public Jove military defection rare enough to make inter-empire news. Ouria was defeated and pursued by Jove forces, and the incident stands as a canonical signal of Third Empire-era internal instability. [Jovian Admiral Ouria goes rogue, is defeated (YC 105)](https://convocation.notion.site/37de035eb05149139ef4a3d1de9a9dfd)

### Drifter Crisis — Final Stages *(YC125)*

Five factions pushed Drifters out of wormhole space and took possession of Drifter pillar formations. This crisis established the occupier list, the destructive-testing summit, and the Jita-gate. It also established the access framework that underlies the Caldari accusation against the Gallente. [New Eden News - Warnings Raised Over Drifter Wormhole Experiments (YC127-10-30)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hPbfRmzc1bI)

## People

### The Deathless

A mysterious figure first surfacing in the YC110 era. Operates the Deathless Network, distributing intel and resources across pirate factions. Currently pulling Archbine away from Mordu's Legion and into his own circle. The modern Deathless arc converges multiple long-running threads: stolen capsuleer-grade resources, an unknown connection to Jove deep history, Pochven operations, the Archbine defection, and the counter-insurgency operations of Intaki Bank and Upwell. The resurrected Sansha Kuvakei. Whether he is truly the original or a constructed simulacrum operating in his name remains unresolved. He coordinates Sansha incursions from the Stain region. Zorya continues to surface as the Triglavian-side speaker through the Pochven and Drifter-conflict cycles. ## Organizations

### Deathless Circle

Air-aligned. The Deathless Circle is absorbing Archbine and operating in Pochven, and is the counter-target of Intaki Bank operations. Multiple lore arcs from the Drifter Crisis onward converge toward it.

### Deathless Network

The operational layer between the Deathless and the broader pirate ecosystem. Functions as a black-market intel exchange, seeding intel and resources — including capsuleer-grade resources — to pirate factions. Intaki Bank's counter-insurgency is aimed at this network. [The Lore You Need For EVE In 2025 - Part 1 - The Deathless](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J8SFloh6Q2s)

### Intaki Bank

A subsidiary financial entity tied to the Syndicate and affiliated with Upwell. Maintains a counter-insurgency posture against the Deathless, but is now structurally compromised by Silphy's arrest and the Syndicate annexation. [Intaki Bank secures R&D funding (YC 109)](http://community.eveonline.com/news/news-channels/world-news/intaki-bank-secures-funding-for-syndicate-r-d-program/) · [Intaki Bank secures funding for Syndicate R&D program](https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/intaki-bank-secures-funding-for-syndicate-rd-program)

### Intaki Syndicate

A loose autonomous federation in the Syndicate region, founded by Caldari-sympathetic Intaki exiles after the Caldari Rebellion. Each station operates autonomously; the Syndicate is officially non-political. The conquest of YC128.05.05 ended its sovereignty, but its membership network continues to operate as a fugitive financial and smuggling structure, with capital ships escaping into highsec. [Silphy en Diabel Arrested in Relation to Jita-gate Incident — New Eden News](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kFLp1gEBqEw) · [The Scope — Intaki Syndicate Fleet Attempts Breakout](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfqboHJUNpw)

### Sleeper Civilization

A lost Jovian-descended civilization in Anoikis wormhole space, awakened during the Apocrypha-era wormhole opening following Seyllin. Structurally tied to the Construct and the Drifter emergence. ### Society of Conscious Thought (SoCT)

An inter-empire research society. Convenes capsuleer briefings on Drifter, Triglavian, and Construct findings; functions as a structural bridge between the four empires on cosmic-scale research. Its briefings at Yulai are canonical reference points for the Drifter Crisis aftermath. ### Third Jovian Empire

The current iteration of the Jovian polity following the Second Empire's collapse — diminished and isolated. Jovian Admiral Ouria's YC105 rogue defection was a Third-Empire-era crisis. The Drifter emergence is the cosmic-scale crisis the Third Empire has not formally claimed.
